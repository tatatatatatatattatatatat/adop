Audi

Audi AG – niemiecki producent samochodów osobowych, z siedzibą w Ingolstadt, należący do koncernu Volkswagen AG. Założony został w 1909 roku przez Augusta Horcha, który założył nową spółkę po tym, jak odszedł z zarządu August-Horch-Motorwagen AG, którą założył w 1899 roku[2]. Nazwa przedsiębiorstwa wywodzi się z gry słów: Audi to łacińskie tłumaczenie nazwiska Horch (oznacza „słuchaj!”). Po wielu sukcesach podczas Międzynarodowego Rajdu Alpejskiego w Austrii Audi zwróciło na siebie uwagę międzynarodową. Audi zwyciężyło w rajdzie trzy razy z rzędu, w latach 1912, 1913 i 1914.[3]

Audi A1

The Audi A1 is a supermini car produced by the German automaker Audi since 2010. It is known for its compact size, stylish design, and advanced technology features. The A1 is available in various trims and engine options, catering to different customer preferences.

Audi A2

The Audi A2 is a compact MPV that was produced by Audi from 1999 to 2005. It was notable for its lightweight aluminum construction, which contributed to its fuel efficiency and performance. The A2 featured a unique design and was available with various engine options, including diesel and petrol variants.

Audi A4

The Audi A4 is a compact executive car that has been in production since 1994. It is known for its blend of performance, luxury, and technology. The A4 is available in sedan and wagon body styles, with a range of engine options, including turbocharged petrol and diesel engines. It features Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system for enhanced traction and handling.

Audi A6

The Audi A6 is a mid-size luxury car that has been in production since 1994. It is known for its sophisticated design, advanced technology, and powerful engine options. The A6 is available in sedan and wagon body styles, with features such as Audi's Virtual Cockpit, MMI infotainment system, and a range of driver assistance technologies.

Audi A8

The Audi A8 is a full-size luxury sedan that has been in production since 1994. It is the flagship model of Audi, known for its opulent interior, cutting-edge technology, and powerful engine options. The A8 features advanced driver assistance systems, a luxurious cabin with high-quality materials, and Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system for superior handling.
